About Competitions

To further support the community in helping aspiring artists find a place to start their career, we also want to organize competitions where aspiring artists can perform and compete.

These competitions can build the confidence of artists as they are performing to audiences and judges. Through these competitions, experts and the audience can provide feedback to the artist, which allows them to improve and try again the next time we organize a competition. Winners of this competition can get the opportunity to work with us.

People who are interested can sign up through our website using a form that will be announced before we organize a competition. People can get to know about the competition through notifications.

Features:

  • Form for people to sign up for competitions
  • Notification to let people know if there is a competition
